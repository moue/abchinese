Read For Me makes learning Chinese easier by letting you paste in Chinese text and get instant pronunciation, phonetic breakdowns, and sharing options.

It is perfect for anyone, especially ABCs (American-born Chinese), who wants to improve Chinese reading and speaking skills.

Step 1: Paste Chinese text into the main input box, or try an example.

Step 2: Click the play button to hear the text spoken aloud for pronunciation practice.

Step 3: Review the breakdown of each character, including pinyin, meaning, and phonetic cues.

Step 4: Use Share to send your result to a friend or save it for later.

Read For Me is useful for learners at any level. If someone is working on Chinese, send it to them.
